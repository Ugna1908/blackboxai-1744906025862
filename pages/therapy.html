Our Therapy Approaches

We believe that each client is unique, and not every approach to therapy works for everyone. Our therapists adopt an eclectic approach, drawing from a diverse range of methodologies.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Helps identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors that affect your emotions and actions.

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Focuses on accepting difficult thoughts and feelings while committing to behaviors that improve and enrich your life.

Dialectical Behavioural Therapy (DBT)

Combines cognitive techniques with mindfulness to help regulate emotions and improve relationships.

Person-Centred Therapy

Creates a supportive environment where you can grow and heal through self-exploration and acceptance.

Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)

Focuses on emotional awareness and expression to help transform problematic emotions and improve relationships.

Exposure Response Prevention Therapy (ERPT)

Particularly effective for anxiety disorders and OCD, involves gradual exposure to feared situations or thoughts.

Mindfulness

Cultivates present-moment awareness and acceptance, reducing stress and enhancing emotional regulation.

Narrative Therapy

Helps you create new meanings and perspectives by examining the stories you tell about yourself and your life.
