Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

24-Hour Notice

We request a 24-hour notice for cancellation or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the slot to someone else who might need it.

Rescheduling

Sessions can be rescheduled once without any charges if done with at least 24 hours' notice. Any subsequent rescheduling may incur a fee.

Late Cancellation

Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged the full session fee. This policy helps us maintain our commitment to all clients.

Late Arrival

If you're running late, please inform us. Sessions cannot be extended beyond the scheduled time, as it may affect other appointments.

Emergencies

We understand that emergencies happen. In case of genuine emergencies, exceptions to the cancellation policy may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
