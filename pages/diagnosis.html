What is Psychological Assessment?

Psychological assessment is a process of testing and evaluation that uses a combination of techniques to help arrive at some hypotheses about a person and their behavior, personality, and capabilities.

Our comprehensive assessments are conducted by qualified Clinical Psychologists and include structured interviews, standardized tests, and detailed observations, tailored to your specific concerns.

The outcome of an assessment is a detailed report that provides clarity about your psychological functioning, a formal diagnosis (if applicable), and recommendations for treatment and support.
