What is Couples Counselling?

Couples counselling is a form of therapy that helps couples of all types recognize and resolve conflicts and improve their relationships. Through counselling, you can make thoughtful decisions about rebuilding and strengthening your relationship or going your separate ways.

Our therapists provide a supportive environment where both partners can express themselves openly and honestly. We help you identify patterns, improve communication, and develop healthier ways of interacting.

Whether you're dealing with serious issues or simply want to strengthen your bond, couples counselling can provide valuable tools and insights for your relationship.

How Couples Counselling Works

1

Initial Assessment

In the first session, your therapist will meet with both of you to understand your relationship history, concerns, and goals for therapy.

2

Joint Sessions

Regular sessions with both partners focus on improving communication, addressing specific issues, and learning new relationship skills.

3

Building New Patterns

With your therapist's guidance, you'll practice new communication techniques and relationship behaviors both in session and at home.

Couples counselling sessions are typically 60 minutes long and held weekly or bi-weekly. The number of sessions varies depending on your specific situation and goals.

All sessions are conducted online via secure video calls for your convenience.
