OUR APPROACH TO THERAPY

We believe that each client is unique, and not every approach to therapy works for everyone. Our therapists adopt an eclectic approach, drawing from a diverse range of methodologies. All the therapists working with us follow a queer-friendly, trauma-informed approach.
